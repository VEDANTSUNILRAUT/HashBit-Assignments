Toyota Supra Mk4

The Toyota Supra Mk4, also known as the Toyota Supra A80, was produced from 1993 to 2002. Engine options include a 2JZ-GE naturally aspirated inline-six engine producing around 220 horsepower and a 2JZ-GTE twin-turbocharged inline-six engine producing around 320 horsepower. It's known for its iconic design, robust performance, and tunability.

Lamborghini Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is a flagship supercar renowned for its aggressive styling and blistering speed. Powered by a V12 engine, the Aventador delivers jaw-dropping performance on both the road and track.

Koenigsegg

Koenigsegg is a Swedish hypercar manufacturer known for producing some of the fastest and most advanced cars in the world. Models like the Koenigsegg Agera, Koenigsegg Jesko, and Koenigsegg Regera are among the most well-known in their lineup.
